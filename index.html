Intro

My name is Jenna Serag and I am a rising Junior at Arizona State University. I am majoring in computer science and am pursuing a Cross Sector Leadership Certificate. My expected graduation date is May 2026.

I am going down the software engineering route as I am interested in web devlepment. I took my first software engineering course and had a blast working on frontend with my team. In my free time. I make jewelry and work on growing my small jewelry business! Dulcet Jewels is one of my proudest achievements and can't wait to see what the future holds for us!

Work

I am currently at the end of my first internship at American Express. I work under the Global Card Services Technology Unit. I love what I do especially when I can help contribute to small-medium sized business! i currently work on the frontend side and have had my first changes go into production.

Earlier this summer, I was a UGTA for CSE301 which is a computing ethics class. I helped guide and support students throughout their session A class as well as answer any questions they have.

Lastly, I have been running everything in my small business on my own for the past 4 years! It takes a lot of time, but is definitely rewarding. I can ensure that I am putting out high quality, handmade jewelry for my customers. I have recently joined StudentMade-ASU which is an organization that provides small businesses with a platform to help reach a wider audience. This opened up opporunities like hosting pop ups on campus and being in the The State Press, and having an article where my jewelry was featured on the front page!
